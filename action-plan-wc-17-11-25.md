# Action Plan to Achieve SMART Deal Sourcing Goals – W/C 17/11/25

This plan breaks down each goal into actionable steps for the week. Review progress daily and update as needed.

## 1. Financial (Target: £2,000 in sourcing fees or new investor commitment)
- Review current pipeline and identify hot leads most likely to close this week.
- Prioritise follow-ups with interested investors and buyers.
- Prepare and send professional deal packs to prospects.
- Schedule calls/meetings to discuss deals and address objections.
- Track all financial activity in the business spreadsheet daily.

## 2. Viewings (3+ property viewings)
- Search Rightmove, Zoopla, and local agent sites for new listings in Cardiff/Barry.
- Contact agents/landlords to book viewings early in the week (Mon/Tue).
- Prepare a checklist for each viewing (key questions, photos, comparables).
- Attend viewings, take detailed notes, and build rapport with agents.
- Summarise each viewing in your tracker the same day.

## 3. Offers (2+ formal offers)
- Analyze viewing notes and run numbers (yield, ROI, comparables).
- Draft offer emails with clear terms and rationale.
- Submit offers within 24 hours of viewing.
- Log all offers and set reminders for follow-up calls.

## 4. Content (1 article/post, 1 highlight/case study)
- Choose a topic relevant to your audience (e.g., “How to Spot a Great HMO Deal”).
- Draft and schedule a LinkedIn post or blog article by midweek.
- Share a recent deal or viewing as a case study on social media.
- Engage with comments and messages to boost reach.

## 5. Network (5 new contacts, 3 follow-ups)
- Identify and connect with new agents, landlords, or sourcers via LinkedIn, WhatsApp, or networking groups.
- Send personalised intro messages and request a call/meeting.
- Follow up with 3+ existing contacts to nurture relationships.
- Log all new contacts and interactions in your CRM or tracker.

## 6. Events (1+ networking event, 2+ new connections)
- Research local property events or online webinars for the week.
- Register and prepare a short intro about your business.
- Attend the event, ask questions, and connect with at least 2 new people.
- Follow up with new connections within 24 hours.

## 7. Training / Learning (1 module/course, summary)
- Select a relevant training (e.g., negotiation, compliance, deal analysis).
- Block out 1-2 hours in your calendar for focused learning.
- Take notes and summarise key takeaways.
- Write a short action plan for applying new knowledge to your business.

---

_Review this plan each morning. Track progress, adjust as needed, and celebrate wins at week’s end!_
