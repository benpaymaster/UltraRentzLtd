# Social Media Plan

## Objectives
- Build brand awareness for UltraRentz Ltd
- Generate leads and engage with property investors, landlords, and co-sourcers
- Share educational and value-driven content

## Platforms
- LinkedIn (primary for B2B and investor networking)
- Facebook (groups, ads, community)
- Instagram (visual property updates, stories)
- X/Twitter (market news, quick updates)
- YouTube (property walkthroughs, educational videos)

## Content Types
- Deal highlights and case studies
- Market insights and news
- Educational posts (e.g., "What is a lease option?")
- Behind-the-scenes/team updates
- Testimonials and success stories
- Video tours and live Q&A

## Posting Frequency
- LinkedIn: 3x/week
- Facebook: 2x/week
- Instagram: 2x/week
- X/Twitter: 3x/week
- YouTube: 2x/month

## Engagement
- Respond to all comments and messages within 24 hours
- Join and contribute to relevant property groups
- Collaborate with influencers and partners
