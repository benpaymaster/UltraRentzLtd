# Business Plan

## Market Opportunity
- Strong demand for HMOs and lease options in South Wales
- Regeneration and economic growth in Cardiff and Barry

## Services
- Deal sourcing (HMO, lease options)
- Investor support and education
- Co-sourcing and JV opportunities

## Operations
