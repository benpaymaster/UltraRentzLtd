# SMART Deal Sourcing Goals – W/C 17/11/25

These public goals are set for accountability, growth, and transparency. Progress will be reviewed at week’s end.

## 1. Financial
- Generate at least £2,000 in sourcing fees or secure a new investor commitment this week.
- Track all income and expenses in the business spreadsheet.

## 2. Viewings
- Book and attend a minimum of 3 property viewings (Cardiff/Barry).
- Write a short summary for each viewing (address, agent, key notes).

## 3. Offers
- Submit at least 2 formal offers (HMO or lease option) based on this week’s viewings.
- Record offer details and set follow-up reminders.

## 4. Content
- Publish 1 new article or LinkedIn post about deal sourcing or market insights.
- Share a property highlight or case study on social media.

## 5. Network
- Add 5 new property professionals (agents, landlords, sourcers) to your network.
- Follow up with at least 3 existing contacts.

## 6. Events
- Attend at least 1 property networking event (in-person or online).
- Connect with at least 2 new people and take notes.

## 7. Training / Learning
- Complete 1 property-related training module or course.
- Summarise key learnings and how they will be applied.

---

_Progress will be tracked and updated publicly. Stay accountable and keep moving forward!_
