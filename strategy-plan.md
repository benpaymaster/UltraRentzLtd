# Strategy Plan

## Sourcing Strategy
- Focus on Cardiff (HMOs) and Barry (lease options)
- Build relationships with local agents, landlords, and investors
- Use direct-to-vendor marketing and online platforms

## Deal Flow
- Target: 4 HMOs and 3 lease options per month
- Qualify deals using strict investment criteria
- Package and present deals to investors

## Growth
- Expand sourcing to nearby towns after 6 months
- Develop co-sourcing and JV partnerships
