# Growth Plan

## Team Development
- Hire/acquire skilled sourcers, analysts, and admin support
- Invest in training (property sourcing, negotiation, compliance)
- Foster a culture of innovation, creativity, and collaboration

## Revenue Expansion
- Increase deal flow (target more areas, new strategies)
- Upsell: offer premium investor services (mentoring, portfolio building)
- Develop partnerships and joint ventures
- Automate lead generation and deal analysis

## Cost Efficiency
- Use technology to automate repetitive tasks
- Outsource non-core activities
- Negotiate better rates with suppliers/partners

## Innovation & Automation
- Regular team brainstorming for new strategies
- Invest in tools for deal analysis, CRM, and marketing automation
- Encourage team to propose and test new ideas

## Acquisition & Market Share
- Expand into new towns/cities every 6-12 months
- Acquire or partner with smaller sourcing firms
- Attend/host industry events to build network
