Selective Licensing

Leeds Selective Licensing requirements for landlords in designated areas.

URGENT 2026

  Designated Areas

If you rent out a property in certain parts of the Armley, Beeston and Holbeck, Burmantofts and Richmond Hill, Hunslet and Riverside, Gipton and Harehills, and Farnley and Wortley wards, you need to apply for a selective licence.

  Key Requirements

Deadline: Apply by 9 February 2026
New Purchases: Apply within 14 days
Failure Penalty: Up to £30,000 per offence
Application Types: Online (£1,100) or Paper (£1,225)

  Fee Structure (from 26 Feb 2026)

Online Applications:
First fee: £400
Second fee: £700 (£550 for LRS members)

Paper Applications:
First fee: £525
Second fee: £700 (£550 for LRS members)

Properties Which Do Not Need a Licence

  • • Holiday lets
  • • Business premises
  • • Socially let properties
  • • Student accommodation owned by a university
  • • Accommodation where the tenant is a family member
  • • Any property where the landlord already holds an HMO licence
  • • Properties that are empty

Application Process

Before Applying

  • • Inform mortgage lender
  • • Notify other owners
  • • Contact property managers
  • • Notify tenants (3+ year leases)

Licence Conditions

  • • Gas safety certificates
  • • Electrical safety
  • • Smoke & CO alarms
  • • Property inspections

Contact Information

Phone: 0113 535 1369
Email: eswselective.licensing@leeds.gov.uk
