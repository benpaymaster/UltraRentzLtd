# Research Plan

## Objective
Identify real estate problems and trends that can be monetised into new projects, startups, tools, or services.

## Approach
- Monitor property forums, news, and social media for pain points
- Survey investors, landlords, and tenants for recurring issues
- Analyze market data for gaps and inefficiencies
- Attend industry events and webinars for inspiration

## Research Activities
- Monthly brainstorming sessions with team
- Track and document all problems/opportunities found
- Prioritise ideas based on market size, urgency, and monetisation potential
- Rapidly prototype and test new solutions

## Example Research Areas
- Automation tools for deal analysis
- Platforms for off-market property matching
- Services for compliance and due diligence
- Solutions for tenant management and retention
